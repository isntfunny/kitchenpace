### Seed Content: Populate with Recipes (Week 2-3)

**Why this matters**: No one wants to join an empty platform. A recipe app needs recipes from day one.

#### Target: 20-30 Quality Recipes at Launch

**Recipe Categories to Include**:

| Category                  | Count | Examples                                                  |
| ------------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| German Classics           | 5-7   | Schnitzel, Sauerbraten, Spätzle, Rouladen, Kartoffelsalat |
| Baking (Backen)           | 4-5   | Apfelstrudel, Schwarzwälder Kirschtorte, Brot, Plätzchen  |
| Quick Meals               | 4-5   | One-pan dinners, 30-min meals                             |
| Meal Prep                 | 3-4   | Weekly prep recipes, batch cooking                        |
| Seasonal                  | 2-3   | Current season highlights                                 |
| Impressive Dinner Parties | 4-5   | Multi-course meals that show off flow diagrams best       |

#### Recipe Selection Criteria

- [ ] Recipes with clear parallel tasks (show off the flow diagram value)
- [ ] Mix of quick (15-30 min) and elaborate (1-2+ hours)
- [ ] Mix of easy and intermediate difficulty
- [ ] Visually appealing - good for screenshots/social
- [ ] German cuisine + international appeal

#### Sources for Recipes

- [ ] Convert public domain German recipes
- [ ] Adapt trusted sources ( cookbook classics)
- [ ] Create original recipes
- [ ] Ask friends/family for family recipes
- [ ] Credit sources transparently

#### Recipe Quality Standards

- [ ] Professional photos (or high-quality AI-generated)
- [ ] Complete ingredient lists with amounts
- [ ] Clear, step-by-step instructions
- [ ] Prep time, cook time, servings noted
- [ ] Tags: difficulty, category, time, cuisine
- [ ] **Converted to flow diagrams** - this is key!

#### Flow Diagram Showcase (5-10 Featured Recipes)

Create premium flow diagrams for these recipes to showcase the feature:

1. 🎄 Christmas Dinner (most complex - show off!)
2. 🥘 Beef Rouladen
3. 🍝 Risotto with asparagus
4. 🥧 Apple strudel
5. 🍖 BBQ pulled pork
6. 🥗 Meal prep Sunday
7. 🎂 Birthday cake

#### For Each Featured Recipe:

- [ ] High-quality hero image
- [ ] Beautiful flow diagram with all lanes populated
- [ ] Full ingredient list
- [ ] Engaging description
- [ ] Tags and category

## How Friends Can Help (No Tech Knowledge Needed)

Assign each friend 1-2 tasks from below. Keep it fun and low-pressure!

### Friend 1: The Social Sharer

**Task**: Share the launch on their personal social media

- [ ] Send them the link + a screenshot
- [ ] They post to Instagram/Facebook/Twitter with a personal message
- [ ] Example: "My friend built this cool app that turns recipes into flow diagrams - had to share!"
- [ ] Ask them to tag you so you can thank them

### Friend 2: The Feedback Giver

**Task**: Use the app and give honest opinions

- [ ] Send them the link
- [ ] Ask them to: Try creating a recipe, browse existing ones, click around
- [ ] Questions to answer:
    - "What was confusing?"
    - "What did you like?"
    - "Would you actually use this?"
    - "What would make it better?"
- [ ] Buy them a coffee for their time ☕

### Friend 3: The Recipe Contributor

**Task**: Share their favorite family recipe

- [ ] Ask: "What's a recipe you make that has lots of steps?"
- [ ] Have them describe it to you (you'll input it)
- [ ] Or: Send you a recipe link and you convert it
- [ ] Credit them as "Recipe contributor" if published

### Friend 4: The Word-of-Mouth Agent

**Task**: Tell 5 people about it

- [ ] Ask them to tell friends, family, coworkers
- [ ] Give them a simple pitch: "It's a recipe app that shows cooking as a flow diagram - helps when you're cooking multiple things"
- [ ] No posting needed, just conversation

### Backup Tasks (If Friends Have More Time)

| Task                 | What They Do                               |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Screenshot collector | Take screenshots of the app on their phone |
| Grammar checker      | Read through recipe descriptions for typos |
| Photo finder         | Find 2-3 public domain photos for recipes  |
| Cheerleader          | Send encouraging messages on launch day    |

### What to Tell Your Friends

> "I'm launching [app name] on [date] and I'd love your help!
>
> You don't need to know anything about tech - I just need people to:
>
> 1. Try it and tell me what they think
> 2. Share it with friends if they like it
> 3. Maybe share one recipe from their family
>
> Should only take 15-30 minutes. I'll owe you a coffee! ☕"
